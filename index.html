ABOUT BARBARA SHEEHAN EVENTS

For the last 25 years, Barbara Sheehan Events (BSE) has combined strategic event planning with stunning creative to help corporations innovate their marketing and activate their brands. Our creative solutions and collaborative approach has made us a trusted advisor with long-standing relationships with our clients.



What We Do Best

Building high profile, customer engagement campaigns that offer extensive PR exposure, relationship building opportunity, and brand positioning is our specialty.



Our Team

Our strength lies in combining on-target communications strategy with fresh creative to create logistically precise productions that deliver big impact. Our full-service, award-winning team includes: brand strategists, communications experts, media specialists, event planners, culinary planners, content creators and designers and production managers.



Why Experience Matters

Events are large financial investments with a million and one details. Precision in execution is critical as mistakes are expensive and in the spotlight. With 25-years experience, and more than 1,000 events produced, we’ve earned our event stripes and are ready to execute for you.

HOW WE WORK

Philosophy

Our business philosophy is simple: offer really creative solutions, execute with precision and get results. And, it’s working. We are the trusted advisor to several top brands who we’ve served for 20 years (but who’s counting?).
