What We Do Best

Building high profile, customer engagement campaigns that offer extensive PR exposure, relationship building, and brand positioning is our specialty.



Our Team

Our event team provides: brand strategy, event planning, graphic and environmental design, full-scale production, contet development, and communications and media planning.



Why Experience Matters

Events are large financial investments with layers of detail. Precision in execution is important because mistakes can be costly and in the spotlight. We've produced over 1,000 events in the last 25 years and are ready to execute for you.

HOW WE WORK

Philosophy

Our business philosophy is simple: exceed the client’s expectations every time, without fail.



Process

The client’s vision is our inspiration! We listen, we collaborate and we develop fresh, on-budget approaches to achieve the desired outcome. Communication is critical for flawless execution. We are in touch every step of the way, charting progress, illustrating with floor plans/event flows, and securing approvals. Our level of service and fee structure is customized to the client’s needs. We work in tandem with the brand team, taking on as much or as little as necessary to succeed.
